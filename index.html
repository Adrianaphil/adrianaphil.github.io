Adriana Alcaraz Sanchez

PhD researcher in Philosopy

Hi! I’m Adriana, an SGSAH-funded PhD researcher in Philosophy at the Centre for the Study of the Perceptual Experience (CSPE), University of Glasgow.

I’m an interdisciplinary researcher and my investigation falls within the intersection of philosophy of mind, psychology and neuroscience. For my PhD thesis, I’m investigating the phenomenology of minimal forms of consciousness during sleep, including dreams and dreamless sleep experiences. In my research I also encourage the dialogue between Western and Eastern philosophical traditions.

I’m eager to connect with other researchers working on the nature of consciousness, altered states of consciousness and cognitive science. Feel free to contact me or reach me out!
